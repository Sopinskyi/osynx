Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary: OsynX Bridge reads Samsara™ data with your token and writes values into your Google Sheets™. OsynX does not run backend servers for your fleet data and does not sell your information.

Data we process

  • Configuration you enter in the add-on (e.g., Samsara™ API token, Org ID) is stored in your Sheet’s document properties.
  • The add-on requests data from Samsara™ on demand over HTTPS and writes results into the active sheet.
  • Basic site analytics may be collected by our hosting provider (e.g., GitHub Pages) per their policy.

Data retention

OsynX does not store fleet data on OsynX servers. Data you see in Google Sheets™ remains under your Google account control.

Security

Your Samsara™ token’s permissions determine what can be read. We recommend read-only tokens and rotating them periodically.

Your choices

  • Revoke or rotate your Samsara™ token at any time.
  • Remove the add-on or clear the Sheet’s properties to delete configuration.
