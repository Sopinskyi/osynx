Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-15

Overview

GSA-X is a Google Sheets™ add-on published by Ochyro Inc. The add-on runs entirely within your Google account. Ochyro Inc does not operate any backend that collects or stores your data.

Data we access

  • Google Sheets content you open the add-on in, to read and write cells per your actions.
  • Document Properties to store your Samsara API token within the Sheet file you configure.
  • External requests to Samsara APIs to fetch locations/ids you request.

Data we store

We (Ochyro Inc) do not store or transmit your data to our servers. Your Samsara token is saved in the active Sheet’s Document Properties and remains under your control. You can remove it at any time by clearing the setting in the sidebar or deleting the document property.

Third-party services

  • Google Workspace APIs (Apps Script, Sheets).
  • Samsara APIs.

Security

We request only the minimum Google OAuth scopes needed for the add-on to function. Access is limited to the active spreadsheet and executing external requests you initiate.
