Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary: GSA-X reads Samsara data with your token and writes values into your Google Sheet. OsynX does not run backend servers for your data and does not sell your information.

Data we process

  • Configuration you enter in the add-on (e.g., Samsara API token, Org ID) is stored in your Sheet’s properties.
  • The add-on requests data from Samsara on demand and writes results into the active sheet.
  • Basic analytics from GitHub Pages (site visits) may be collected by GitHub; see their policy.

Data retention

OsynX does not store fleet data on OsynX servers. Data you see in Sheets is under your Google account control.

Security

Your Samsara token permissions determine what can be read. We recommend read-only tokens and rotating them periodically.

Your choices

  • Revoke the Samsara token anytime.
  • Remove the add-on or clear the Sheet properties to delete configuration.
